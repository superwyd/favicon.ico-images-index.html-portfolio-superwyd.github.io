Final Randoms


Graphic Design

Dec 2016

A graphic design in few minutes during the final exam week.

Inspiration

Final week is always filled with large number of exams, final projects, and paper. Coffee is quite necessary under the great stress during this week. While taking a Computer Science course named Data mining and machine learning, our final project is designing a Pacman game and making it earn higher scores. In order to make my pacman work better, I played this game for many times.

While making the pacman eating more pac-dots, I thought I was just like this pacman. But instead of eating pac-dots to survive, I had to drink a lot of coffee to survive from final week.

~ Handmade by Yudi Wang with Bootstrap ~
